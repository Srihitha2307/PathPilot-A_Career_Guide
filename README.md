## Problem Statement

## Key Challenges
- Matching skills, subjects, and interests to suitable careers  
- Researching scattered and unreliable information sources  
- Limited access to curated guidance on colleges, courses, resources, and internships  
- Uncertainty about costs, difficulty levels, and future prospects

## Project Objectives (Summary)
- Provide personalized career path recommendations  
- Offer detailed guidance on colleges, courses, costs, and job prospects  
- Reduce research time with curated resources and verified links  
- Help students make confident, informed decisions
